Privacy Policy

Effective February 26, 2026

BendBSN ("we", "our", or "the platform") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using BendBSN, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the platform:

Category Examples How Collected
Account data Email address, display name, role (student/RN/instructor), year level Registration form or Google Sign-In
Authentication data Firebase UID, login timestamps, IP address (hashed) Automatically on sign-in
Educational content Nursing notes, head-to-toe assessments, screening tool responses you save When you save documents in the app
Presence data Online/away status, last active timestamp Automatically while logged in
Chat and community content Messages in channels, direct messages, community posts When you post or send messages
Device data Browser type, FCM push token (if notifications enabled) Automatically when you enable push notifications

We do not knowingly collect real patient data or Protected Health Information (PHI). All documentation you create on BendBSN should use fictional or de-identified practice data only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service — authenticate your account, store your saved notes, enable chat and community features
  • Send welcome and account emails — one-time welcome email sent to new accounts via EmailJS
  • Push notifications — send you alerts for direct messages and @mentions if you enable them
  • Platform security — detect abuse, enforce bans, maintain login history for administrators
  • Improve the platform — understand how features are used (no third-party analytics trackers)

We do not sell your information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

BendBSN uses the following third-party services to operate. Your data is processed and stored by these providers subject to their own privacy policies:

Service Provider What They Process Privacy Policy
Firebase Authentication Google LLC Email, password hash, UID, login history firebase.google.com/support/privacy
Firebase Realtime Database Google LLC Your saved notes, profile, chat messages, presence Same as above
Firebase Cloud Messaging Google LLC Push notification tokens (if enabled) Same as above
Firebase Hosting Google LLC Web traffic logs Same as above
EmailJS EmailJS Ltd Name and email for welcome messages emailjs.com/legal/privacy-policy
Google Apps Script Google LLC User roster management (admin use) policies.google.com/privacy

All Firebase data is stored in Google's us-central1 (Iowa, USA) region.

4. Data Retention

  • Saved documents: Retained until you delete them or your account is closed
  • Chat messages: Retained until deleted by an administrator or by you; channels retain the last 50 messages in the client
  • Login history: Retained for up to 200 entries per user; visible to administrators
  • Presence data: Active session data only; stale entries cleaned up automatically every 30 minutes
  • Push tokens: Retained until you revoke permission or the token becomes invalid

5. Administrator Access

Platform administrators can view:

  • User account information (name, email, role)
  • Login history (timestamps and IP data)
  • Community posts and chat channels (for moderation)
  • Online presence status

Administrators cannot view your saved private nursing notes or direct messages.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can view your saved documents and profile within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your saved notes from within the app; contact an administrator to request full account deletion
  • Push notifications: You can disable push notifications in your browser settings at any time
  • Dark mode and preferences: All UI preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage and never sent to our servers

7. Children's Privacy

BendBSN is intended for use by nursing students and healthcare professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from persons under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including Firebase security rules that restrict data access to authenticated users and role-based permissions. No system is perfectly secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the platform. Continued use after notice of changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact the platform administrator through the BendBSN app.
