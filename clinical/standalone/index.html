Mini-Cog — Patient Cognitive Assessment

Rapid dementia screen (~3 minutes). Three steps: register three words, draw a clock, recall the words.

Step 1 — 3-Word Registration

Ask the resident to listen carefully, remember, and repeat back: Ocean · Desk · Tractor

Step 2 — Clock Drawing Test

Instruct the resident to draw the face of a clock, including the numbers, with hands pointing to 8:20. Instructions may be repeated, but no additional help should be given. Move on after 3 minutes if incomplete.

Sketch what the resident drew (optional)

Step 3 — Word Recall

Ask the resident to repeat the 3 previously presented words (Ocean, Desk, Tractor).

Normal = all numbers depicted once each, in correct sequence and position, hands readably displaying the requested time.

— Mini-Cog Total (0–5) Not scored

0–2 = Positive screen for dementia · 3–5 = Negative screen
